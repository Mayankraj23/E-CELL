about us

Vision

We, here at Entrepreneurship-Cell IIT Jodhpur, share a common belief that revolutionary changes can be brought about in India by encouraging youth to think and perform past the conventional protocols. We emphasize on bringing out the Entrepreneurial skills in individuals and create an independent mindset to think out of the box.

Mission

We focus on cultivating startups from our institute as well as from jodhpur. Jodhpur has a wide potential to become the next business hub. We are to create a highly functional business ecosystem here at Jodhpur. We aim at establishing a mutually beneficial network and collaborations with Startups, B- School, Universities, E-Cells, Investors, establish a national reach.

welcome to E-Cell IITJ

Entrepreneurship-Cell IIT Jodhpur is a student body that focuses on developing and promoting entrepreneurial eco-system and start-up skills in creative and potential minds. It focuses on various dimensions of corporate world that revolves around an entrepreneur. Being a student run body, we educate people about different aspects of a business, guide and promote entrepreneurial ideas and provide resources to help them establish, nurture and run their own startup.

We are a medium to link the entrepreneurs to be with government schemes, angel investors, incubators, venture capitalists etc. We conduct a lot of activities that help us interact with people closely engaged in business sphere, we conduct workshops, ted talk seminars, guest lectures, various competitions, we have our annual Business & Startup Carnival ‘IDEA SPARK’ and much more. We have helped setting up a number of startups successfully.


Join us, and with mutual sharing we will grow together !
